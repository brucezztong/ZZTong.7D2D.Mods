This modlet attempts to fix up the Vanilla water system so that survivalist
can carry water away from water sources, but still kind of preserves water
scaricity in the first two days by taking away the starting food and water.
This mostly allows play to continue for those who don't want to have a
survival game where you are forced to depend on a Trader.

Changes drinking water from a water source into giving a jar of murky water.

Adds a recipe for a dew collector that operate without a filter but give out
murky water.

Adds a recipe for crafting a filter.
