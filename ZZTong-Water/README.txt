This modlet attempts to fix up the Vanilla water system so that survivalist
can carry water away from water sources. You no longer drink directly from
lakes and rivers. Instead you get a jar of murky water which you could drink.

Murky Water now only stacks to 10, like other forms of water.

Boiled Water now has a 5% chance of dysentery. This represents the process
of boiling water is not a perfect method for making potable water.

Changes Pure Mineral Water so that it can only be crafted at a Chemistry
Station, and this represents truly potable water.
